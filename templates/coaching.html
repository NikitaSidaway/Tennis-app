Our Team & Philosophy

Founded in 2001, the Meredith Brothers Tennis Academy has grown into one of New Zealand’s leading training centres, shaping the nation’s top young talent. With over 30 years in the NZ tennis scene, brothers Matt, James, and Tim Meredith bring over 75 years of combined experience in playing and coaching at national and international levels. Their expertise continues to inspire the next generation of players. The team has a wealth of knowledge and experience both playing and coaching

James Meredith

James is our Head Coach at the Meredith Brothers Tennis Academy. He is also our junior administration and has had a very distinguished tennis career. Ranking the 23rd best player in Division 1 Universities in the USA.

Dave Meredith

Dave is a Senior Coach at the Meredith Brothers Academy, leading our Hot Shots programme. Dave is father to Matt, Tim and James responsible for coaching and developing the 3 boys.

Matthew Meredith

Matt is the Director of Coaching at the Meredith Brothers Tennis Academy. He has had over 20 years of tennis coaching experience and is currently the Tennis Canterbury Regional Head Coach.
